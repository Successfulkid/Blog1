How to get waves process rules!

Steps to get "Sea Sick" waves

  • Get a short haircut and edge up.
  • Brush your hair for 1-2 minutes with a wave-brush.
  • Put a washcloth in running hot water, squeeze some water from the rag, and then place hot wash cloth on top of your hair and wipe down.
  • Repeat hot water process for the sides and back of hair also.
  • Place a wave cap onto your head for one day, preferably only at bedtime.
